About AI Healthcare Assistant

Our mission is to leverage artificial intelligence to provide accessible healthcare solutions for everyone.

Project Overview

The AI Healthcare Assistant is designed to help users manage their health by providing symptom analysis, medication tracking, and personalized diet recommendations. Our goal is to empower individuals to take control of their health and make informed decisions.

Technologies Used

We utilize a combination of modern web technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python with Flask for the backend. Our AI models are built using machine learning algorithms to provide accurate health insights.
